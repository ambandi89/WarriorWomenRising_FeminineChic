About Ashley and Kafi

Your Hosts

Ashley Miller

Ashley spent over 10 years in the Army as an Engineer Officer, leading soldiers from platoon level all the way to company command. Her favorite and most meaningful chapter? Commanding the Wounded Warrior Battalion at Fort Bragg, where she supported injured service members as they healed and stepped into their next chapter of life.

Today, she's traded military boots for business attire as an executive recruiter at Spencer Stuart, helping place C-suite tech leaders and championing diverse talent through the firm’s Veteran and Latinx affinity groups.

Ashley now calls Atlanta home, where life revolves around her family! Spending time with her partner, their giggly baby girl, and her sidekick ace the dachshund.

Kafi A. Joseph

Kafi—rhymes with taffy—served as a Military Police Officer on Active Duty before transitioning to Logistics in the National Guard. She led soldiers as a platoon leader, company commander, and operations officer, serving in South Korea, Iraq, and across the U.S. Her favorite assignment? Being a Platoon Leader with the legendary 3rd U.S. Infantry Regiment, The Old Guard.

After the military, Kafi built a powerhouse civilian career with 15+ years in strategy, operations, and program management across public service, energy, and defense. She’s led major initiatives at Accenture, Booz Allen, and Fairbanks Morse—driving growth, innovation, and real-world impact.

Today, she’s the founder of inRG.Design, where she’s helping people rethink productivity using Human Design tools. In short: Kafi blends leadership, intuition, and strategy like nobody else!
